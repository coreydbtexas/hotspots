Gain Precision, Control and Versatility

Managing ingredients for multiple products and recipes is a never-ending balancing act. The HorizonPSI Intelligent Ingredient System takes a matrix-based approach to controlling and storing ingredients, allowing you to work more efficiently, safely and with less downtime.

By integrating “gain in weight” and “loss in weight” logic every time an RBIC, bulk bag, bin or hand station is used, you can ensure precision in your process – all while tracking for GFSI and FSMA compliance. It also keeps combustible dust, labor-intensive housekeeping and your operational footprint to a minimum while a web-based reporting system makes it easy for you to manage and monitor everything in one convenient place.
